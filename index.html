Berserker 3.28 Mirage - Verified Current

Chain Hook Rage Vortex

Leap Slam for mobility, Chain Hook as the main clear/bossing skill, stacking Rage for the attack speed/damage/movement curve and near-permanent Berserk uptime. Double Lucky Block, Fortify, and Determination armour carry the defense layer.

This is a real, currently-ranked build (Tier list pick #13 for 3.28 Mirage) - not a theorycraft. It's been shown clearing Uber Shaper on SSF gear, with flexible tree pathing and low day-one gearing requirements.
